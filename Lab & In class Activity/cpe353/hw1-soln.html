b) Remove the most significant variable in a) and refit the model.
How do the model coefficients and your conclusion about the significance of variables change compared to a) ?

Solution b)¶

Remove TAR and refit. Now Nicotine and Weight have positive coefficients compared to the first model.
But only Nicotine is significant and the $R^2$ reduces somewhat.
The changes in the coefficient values and significances are due to correlation
between TAR and Nicotine as shown in the scatter diagram and correlation matrix.
